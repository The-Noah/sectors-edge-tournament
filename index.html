Sector's Edge Tournament

We will be running a tournament on the 4th of April at 7pm GMT!

Info

It will be a knockout tournament, where each round is best-of-three on three different maps, on servers chosen for ping fairness.

As we are streaming the tournament on Twitch and will use recorded footage to advertise the game, we have decided to run the Salvage game mode as it better showcases the game mechanics and maps.

Rewards

10000 for each player in the winning team.
5000 for each player in the runner-up team.

Register your team of 4, or sign up individually and we will place you in a team: sectorsedge.com/tournament.

See you all at the Salvage Cup! May the best team win!
